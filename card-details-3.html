Sarah Hoffe, MD

Chief Medical Officer, MyCareGorithm, LLC

Dr. Hoffe has been on the faculty of Moffitt Cancer Center for 13 years, specializing in the treatment of radiation for gastrointestinal (GI) cancers. Her peer reviewed publications and research efforts have focused primarily on improving the outcomes of patients with pancreatic, esophageal, and rectal cancers. During that time, she has always been especially interested in education, serving as ASTRO’s Director of E Contouring at the national level, and serving as a Professional Development Coach and leadership module co-director at the University of South Florida (USF) Morsani College of Medicine. Dr. Hoffe has worked with Dr. Harrison nationally to promote leadership education within ASTRO as well, creating the first ever physician led educational panel sessions on this topic. She currently has been focusing on developing leadership curricula for Moffitt’s radiation oncology residents. Dr. Hoffe also devotes significant time to innovation, currently working on a virtual reality prototype to help patients understand the process of MRI guided radiation. Her past work has included being the head writer of a 360-video project for patients with GI cancers which won two Telly awards.
