Jennifer Greenman

Chief Information Officer of CTCA

Greenman obtained an MBA from the University of Florida and undergraduate degrees in Management Information Systems and Finance from Florida State University. She is a member of the College of Healthcare Information Management Executives and was recognized by the Tampa Bay Business Journal as a finalist for the "CIO of the Year Award" in 2016 and 2018. Prior to CTCA, Jennifer served as CIO at Moffitt Cancer Center and Bayfront Health System.
