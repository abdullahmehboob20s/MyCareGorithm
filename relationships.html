Unified Field

Unified Field is a creative innovation and experience design firm. They design and build compelling media experiences in public spaces, the built environment, mobile and online for Fortune 500 companies, and major healthcare and cultural institutions. The experiences they develop convey knowledge, build community and create enduring relationships. They strategically position clients for the future and brings big ideas to life.

Beyond the White Coat

Beyond the White Coat (BTWC) is a healthcare education company with a mission to develop innovative educational materials that will enhance understanding of ways to prevent, diagnose or treat illness. Started by a radiation oncologist, BTWC aspires to bridge the gap between the concerned patient/parent/caregiver and a fully empowered/knowledgeable healthcare advocate. Content for multiple platforms is being created including Virtual Reality (VR) with the first VR children’s book in a series under development. The goal of the VR children’s series is to enhance the conversation between parent and child with important health topics, catalyzed by immersing the reader into an interactive environment where the story comes to life. BTWC produces novel content and partners with other organizations to enhance their content to optimize the patient/parent/caregiver experience.
