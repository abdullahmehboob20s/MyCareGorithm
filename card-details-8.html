Kedar Kirtane, MD

Assistant Member at Moffitt Cancer Center in the Department of Head and Neck-Endocrine Oncology.

Assistant Member at Moffitt Cancer Center in the Department of Head and Neck-Endocrine Oncology. Dr. Kirtane received his MD degree from The University of Florida College of Medicine. He completed an Internal Medicine Residency at Duke University Medical Center. Dr. Kirtane completed a Hematology/Oncology Fellowship at the University of Washington and a Post-Doctoral Fellowship in Oncology at Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center. His clinical focus is patients with head and neck-endocrine cancers.
