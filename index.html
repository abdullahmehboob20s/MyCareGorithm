Transforming the Doctor/Patient Experience

MyCareGorithm was created with the singular purpose of developing the tools to transform the consultation encounter between a cancer patient and a cancer specialist. Our intent is to replace the status quo with a multimedia, educational, impactful, personalized, and visually impressive experience. Our goal is for the patient and their caregivers to emerge with the knowledge and confidence that they understand their cancer journey and are on the path to their best possible outcome…….and, for the cancer specialist and their institutions to provide a differentiating experience for their patients.

Why MyCareGorithm

Why MyCareGorithm?

Cancer patients and their loved ones are scared, vulnerable and at a crossroads in their life. Yet at this fragile moment, they need to make crucial decisions about their care. Most cancer patients do not truly understand the details and complexity of their situation, which are essential ingredients to making good medical decisions. Furthermore, data has shown that most patients forget a significant percentage of what they are told during their cancer consultation.

Our tools allow the multidisciplinary cancer specialists (surgical oncologists, radiation oncologists, and medical oncologists) to create a personalized and curated discussion which brings to a new level, both patient understanding of their diagnosis and their treatment options.
